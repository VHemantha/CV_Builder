Best Practices for Resume Writing in 2026:

To create the most effective resume using our CV builder free tool, follow these expert tips:

  • Use action verbs to describe your accomplishments (achieved, led, developed, increased)
  • Quantify your achievements with numbers and percentages
  • Tailor your resume to each job application
  • Keep it concise - aim for 1-2 pages maximum
  • Use a clean, professional font and consistent formatting
  • Include relevant keywords from the job description
  • Proofread carefully for grammar and spelling errors
